# Olist Sales Analysis & Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyses the **Olist e-commerce dataset** to understand sales performance, customer behavior, category trends, and regional performance. The project combines **Python-based exploratory analysis** with a **Power BI dashboard** to present both detailed insights and a manager-friendly summary view.

The final output includes:

* a **Power BI dashboard** for KPI and visualization reporting,
* a **Jupyter notebook** for data cleaning and analysis,
* and a **business insights report** summarizing key findings and recommendations.

---

## Project Objectives

The main goals of this project were to:

* Analyse Olist’s sales and revenue performance
* Identify top-performing product categories
* Study monthly sales trends and seasonality
* Compare sales across states/regions
* Understand customer review patterns
* Build an interactive dashboard with clear KPIs and insights

## Business Insights

Based on the analysis, the following major business insights were identified:

### 1. A few product categories drive most of the revenue

Customer demand is concentrated in specific categories, meaning a small number of product segments contribute a large share of overall sales.

**Recommendation:**
Focus inventory planning, promotions, and marketing efforts on the top-performing categories.

---

### 2. Sales vary noticeably across months

Monthly sales patterns show that one or more months perform significantly better than others, suggesting seasonality or the impact of campaigns and shopping behavior.

**Recommendation:**
Replicate strategies used during the best-performing month in weaker months to improve consistency in sales.

---

### 3. Revenue is concentrated in certain states/regions

Some regions contribute much more revenue than others, indicating uneven market penetration and stronger demand in selected locations.

**Recommendation:**
Strengthen presence in high-performing states while using targeted campaigns and service improvements to grow weaker regions.

---

### 4. Customer review patterns reveal satisfaction trends

Review scores provide useful insight into customer experience, with higher ratings reflecting stronger satisfaction and lower ratings potentially indicating issues in product quality, delivery, or listing accuracy.

**Recommendation:**
Investigate low-rated orders to identify recurring service or product-related problems.

---

### 5. Category performance changes over time

Some product categories perform better in specific months, showing that category demand is not constant throughout the year.

**Recommendation:**
Use category-level monthly trends for demand forecasting, stock planning, and seasonal promotions.

---

## Most Surprising Finding

One of the most surprising findings from the project was that revenue was not evenly spread across all categories and regions. Instead, a few categories and a few states contributed a disproportionately large share of total sales. This highlights both an opportunity and a risk: while strong-performing segments can be scaled further, overdependence on a small number of categories or markets can limit long-term stability if demand shifts.

## Project Workflow

The project was completed in the following stages:

1. **Data Cleaning & Preparation**

   * cleaned and prepared the sales dataset
   * handled missing values and formatted time-related columns
   * created analysis-ready features such as month and revenue measures

2. **Exploratory Data Analysis in Python**

   * analysed category sales, order patterns, and review distributions
   * explored regional and temporal sales trends
   * identified patterns later used in dashboard creation

3. **Dashboard Development in Power BI**

   * built KPI cards for business summary
   * created charts for category, month, state, and review analysis
   * designed a one-page dashboard with a separate insights summary page

4. **Business Insight Reporting**

   * converted findings into business-focused insights
   * added actionable recommendations based on analysis
